OUR STORY

At Royal Butta LLC, we believe every celebration deserves a touch of magic. Our journey began in 2015 with a simple idea: to make event planning stress-free and unforgettable for everyone. What started as a small family business renting out throne chairs has grown into a full-service party and event rental company, offering everything from balloon decor and photo booths to catering services and wedding essentials.

Over the years, we’ve had the honor of being part of countless birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and community gatherings. Our passion for creativity and attention to detail has earned us the trust of our clients, and we take pride in turning their visions into reality.

Whether it’s a grand wedding or an intimate birthday party, our mission is to provide high-quality rentals and exceptional service that leave lasting memories. At Royal Butta LLC, we don’t just rent items—we create experiences.
